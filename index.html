Hi, I'm Lincoln Ngugi, a passionate photographer with a love for capturing everyday moments in extraordinary ways. My journey began with a simple film camera at the age of 14, and since then, I've grown to specialize in portrait and street photography. Through my lens, I aim to tell authentic stories and preserve fleeting emotions. When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new cities and chasing golden hour light. Photography, for me, is not just a profession — it's a way of seeing the world."
